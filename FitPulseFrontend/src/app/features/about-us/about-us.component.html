About FitPulse

FitPulse is a comprehensive platform that allows individuals to keep track of their health and fitness activities. Through our application, users can log their workouts and health metrics, monitoring their progress over time.

Our Story

FitPulse was founded in 2024 by a group of fitness enthusiasts with a passion for technology and health. Our mission is to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone. With FitPulse, we aim to create a supportive community where individuals can achieve their fitness goals together.
